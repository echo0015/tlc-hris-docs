Mobile App/OBT Requests

✈️OBT Requests

File and track your own Official Business Travel requests.

Rules & Validation

  • Travel start/end date, destination, purpose, mode of transportation, estimated expenses — all required.
  • If you have no immediate head assigned, your request skips the head-approval step and goes straight to HR.
  • If HR assigns you a head while your request is still awaiting HR, it will automatically require that head's approval first before HR can finalize it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about OBT Requests.

Do I need to add accompanying staff?⌄

No, that field is optional — only fill it in if colleagues are traveling with you on the same trip.

Can I edit my OBT request after submitting it?⌄

You can cancel it yourself while it is still Pending; once approved, contact HR for any correction.

I have no immediate head — who approves my request?⌄

It goes straight to HR. If you're later assigned a head while your request is still pending HR review, it will then need that head's approval before HR can finalize it.
