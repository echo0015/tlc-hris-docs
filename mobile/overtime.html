Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Overtime Requests.

Can I cancel a submitted overtime request?⌄

Yes, while it is still Pending.

Why do I need to enter both time in and time out?⌄

Requested hours are calculated from the actual time range you worked, so both are required.
