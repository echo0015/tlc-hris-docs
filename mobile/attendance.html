Your own daily time-in/time-out logs for a selected date range.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about My Attendance.

Why does my attendance show no records for a date range?⌄

It only shows dates where a time-in/time-out was captured by the attendance device — no record means nothing was logged for that day.

Does this include my overtime hours?⌄

No — this shows your regular daily time logs. Overtime is tracked separately under Overtime Requests.
