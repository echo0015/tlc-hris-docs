Web App/CTO (Compensatory Time-Off)

🔁CTO (Compensatory Time-Off)

Lets an employee convert rendered overtime into an equivalent block of time off, subject to the same approval chain.

Rules & Validation

  • Work rendered dates (overtime start/end) and time-off dates (start/end) — both required; end dates must be on or after their respective start dates.
  • Both the work-rendered days and the time-off days support a day-by-day list with individual time ranges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about CTO (Compensatory Time-Off).

What's the difference between CTO and regular Overtime?⌄

Overtime requests compensation for extra hours worked. CTO instead exchanges rendered overtime for an equivalent block of time off.

Can I file a CTO request without prior worked overtime?⌄

No — a CTO request must reference the overtime dates actually worked (Work Rendered) that the time off is being exchanged for.
