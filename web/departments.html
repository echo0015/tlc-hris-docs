🏢Departments

Departments group employees for reporting and are tagged with a color used throughout lists, calendars, and reports.

Rules & Validation

  • Department code — required, must be unique.
  • Department name — required.
  • Badge color — required (pick a preset or enter a custom hex color).

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Departments.

How is a department code different from a college code?⌄

A Department is the top-level organizational grouping (e.g. Higher Education, Basic Education). A College is an academic unit that belongs to a department — see Colleges.

Can I delete a department that already has employees in it?⌄

The system is built around editing rather than deleting departments in active use — reassign or archive affected records first, and consult HR before removing a department with existing history.
