Web App/My Profile

👤My Profile

Every signed-in user's own account settings.

Rules & Validation

  • Current password — required to change your password.
  • New password — required, minimum 6 characters, and must be confirmed (typed twice).

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about My Profile.

I forgot my password. What do I do?⌄

If you can't sign in at all, ask an HR administrator to reset it from User Accounts. If you're signed in and just want to change it, use the form here.

Can I change my own username?⌄

No — username is an HR-managed field. Contact HR if it needs to change.
