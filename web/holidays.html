🎌Holidays & Non-Working Days

Configures company holidays used by attendance calculations and the leave calendar.

Rules & Validation

  • Holiday name — required, max 150 characters.
  • Holiday date — required, valid date.
  • Holiday type — required.
  • If the holiday only applies to part of a day, start time / end time become required, in HH:MM (24-hour) format.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Holidays & Non-Working Days.

Does a holiday automatically affect attendance and leave calculations?⌄

Yes — configured holidays feed into attendance calculations and are reflected on the Leave Calendar.

What's the difference between a whole-day and time-range holiday?⌄

A whole-day holiday covers the entire date; a time-range holiday only applies within the specified start/end time, e.g. a half-day observance.
