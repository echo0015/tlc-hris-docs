Web App/Daily Attendance

🕒Daily Attendance

The daily time-in/time-out log sourced from the biometric/attendance device, used for attendance summaries and payroll.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Daily Attendance.

Where does attendance data come from?⌄

It's synced from the school's biometric/attendance device logs, not entered manually in the normal flow.

What if a time-in/out looks wrong?⌄

Report it to HR/IT so they can check the device log and correct the record if needed.
