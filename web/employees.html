Web App/Employee Management

👥Employee Management

The core HR module: the master record for every employee, from personal details to employment history. Access to add, edit, or export records depends on the signed-in user's permissions.

Rules & Validation

  • Last name / First name — required, max 100 characters.
  • Gender — required, must be Male or Female.
  • Date of birth — required, valid date.
  • Place of birth — required, max 255 characters.
  • Civil status — required, must be Single or Married.
  • Emergency contact (name / number) and cell number — required, max 20 characters for numbers.
  • Address (street / barangay / city / province) — all required, max 255 characters each.
  • Date employed — required, valid date.
  • Employment status, college/department, position — required; position must exist in the Company Positions table.
  • Employee number (when updated separately) — required, must be unique across all employees, max 50 characters.
  • Bulk upload file — required, must be .xlsx or .xls, max size 10 MB.
Deleting an employee outright is intentionally restricted — records are managed through employment status changes (e.g. Resigned, Terminated, Retired) rather than permanent deletion, to preserve historical attendance, leave, and payroll data.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Employee Management.

Why can't I delete an employee record?⌄

TLC-HRIS deliberately avoids hard-deleting employee records to preserve attendance, leave, and payroll history. Instead, change the employee's Employment Status (e.g. Resigned, Terminated) to reflect that they are no longer active.

What file format does Bulk Upload accept?⌄

Excel files only — .xlsx or .xls, up to 10 MB. Always start from the Download Template button on the Employee List page so your columns match what the importer expects.

Why can't I see the Add or Edit button?⌄

Those actions are gated by permissions. Ask an HR administrator to check your role under User Types & Permissions.
