Web App/OBT (Official Business Travel)

✈️OBT (Official Business Travel)

For employees traveling on official college business — tracks destination, purpose, cost, and who else is traveling.

Rules & Validation

  • Travel start / end date — required, valid dates.
  • Destination (city/province), purpose of travel, mode of transportation — all required.
  • Estimated total expenses — required, numeric.
  • Travel schedule — day-by-day breakdown of the trip; accompanying staff are optional.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about OBT (Official Business Travel).

Do I need approval before traveling?⌄

Yes — like other requests, OBT goes through the Immediate Head → HR approval chain before it is finalized.

Can I add someone else as accompanying staff?⌄

Yes, the filing form has an optional field for listing accompanying staff on the same trip.
