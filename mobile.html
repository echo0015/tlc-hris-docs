Mobile App Documentation

How employees use the TLC-HRIS mobile app, split by screen. Pick a screen below for screenshots, step-by-step guidance, validation rules, and frequently asked questions.

Getting Started

🔐

Login

Sign in with the same username and password used on the web app.

 🏠

Home / Dashboard

The first tab you land on after signing in — a snapshot of what needs your attention.

Requests

📥

Requests Hub

The second tab in the bottom navigation — a central menu linking to all four self-service request types.

 🗓️

Leave Requests

File and track your own leave requests, and check your remaining leave credits.

 ⏱️

Overtime Requests

File and track your own overtime requests.

 🔁

CTO Requests

File and track your own Compensatory Time-Off requests.

 ✈️

OBT Requests

File and track your own Official Business Travel requests.

Attendance

🕒

My Attendance

Your own daily time-in/time-out logs for a selected date range.

 📆

My Weekly Schedule

Your assigned work days and hours for the current schedule period.

Profile Hub

The fourth tab in the bottom navigation — links to your profile, requests history, and account settings.

 ⚙️

My Account & Settings

View your account info, switch appearance, change your password, or sign out.

 🪪

My Employee Profile

A read-only view of your official HR record.

 📄

My Profile Requests

Track the status of every profile-change request you have submitted.
